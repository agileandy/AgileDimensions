About Us

Agile Dimensions were established in 2012 with the aim to bring an agile mindset to the Perth market, and since then we have worked with global organisations in the mining, oil & gas, and finance sectors, as well as government organisations.

Our Principal Consultant - Andy Spamer

Andy has been a project manager since 1998, and has always been a firm believer in the PRINCE2 project management methodology. However he was always a pragmatist and he quickly found that for the technology projects he was working on, PRINCE2 was too bureaucratic, and this led him to create his own cut down version - Pragmatic PRINCE. Leading a large complex software project in 2004, Andy discovered Scrum. He worked with his team to try this approach, and it become clear this was the missing piece of his jigsaw. He honed his agile skills and knowledge for the next 6 years before emigrating to Australia in 2010. Andy has continued to develop his skills, growing from a Scrum Master to an Agile Coach, and Business Transformation Consultant.
